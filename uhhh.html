Payton Lee

Payton Lee was once a very curious woman who dabbled in the ball licking. After about 3 years of continously attending ball licking conventions, she met a man named Elliot. Ever since he said, "I think you're pretty", she was heartstruck and could never leave his arms. Payton vowed to never attend another ball licking convention as long as Elliot was still in her life. It has since been 6 months since Payton's last ball licking convention.
